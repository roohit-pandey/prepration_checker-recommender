#Placement Preparation Helper

## What It Does

This project is a tool that helps students get ready for job interviews. It asks them a questions sees how they do and tells them what topics they should study based on how well they did and what they are not good at.

---

## What You Can Do With It

* Take a quiz with many questions

* Get a score

* Find out your skill level like if you're a beginner, intermediate or advanced

* See how you did on each topic

* Get personalized advice on what to study

* Find out what you are not good at

---

* Python

#. Answer the questions that appear on the screen

## How It Works

* The program asks you questions that have answers to choose from

* Each question is about a topic

* If you answer correctly:

* You get points for each answer

* The program figures out your skill level

* Then the program:

* Tells you what topics you should study based on your level

* Shows you what topics you are not good at

---

## What You Might See

* Your score, like 5 out of 8

* Your level, like intermediate

* What topics you should study, like Stack, Queue, Sorting

* What you are not good at, like Graphs, Recursion

---

## What It Cannot Do

* It only has a fixed set of questions

* It does not remember what you did before

* It gives you advice based on rules not on what it learns from you
